## Voice Detection

The system detects gender from character names:

**Male Voice Keywords:**
- bheem, satyam, kevin, john, david, michael
- mr., sir, male

**Female Voice Keywords:**
- kalyani, flavia, priya, sarah
- ms., mrs., miss, female

**Example:**
- `Bheem, Satyam` â†’ Both male names â†’ **Male voice**
- `Kalyani, Priya` â†’ Both female names â†’ **Female voice**
- `Bheem, Kalyani` â†’ First is male â†’ **Male voice** (first wins)
